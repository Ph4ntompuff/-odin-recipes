Cheese Ravioli

Image Source

Description: Raviolis filled with cheese topped with a sauce of one's choice

Ingredients

  • Cheese (We went with parmesan and mozarrella)
  • Tomato Sauce (can be store bought or made at home)
  • Flour, salt, eggs, water, and olive oil to make the dough

Directions

  1. Make and knead dough for the raviolis, make into a ball, and then wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes
  2. After time has passed, roll dough into sheets
  3. Add cheese as filling to the pasta followed by adding another layer of dough
  4. Cut out individual raviolis
  5. Cook the raviolis in water until they rise to the top and the insides are hot
  6. Serve with sauce of choice
