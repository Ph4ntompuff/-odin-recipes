French Toast

Image Source

Description: Delicious French Toast covered in sugar and cinnamon and then fried

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Some heavy cream or milk
  • 1 or 2 tablespoon of sugar
  • A tablespoon of cinammon
  • Bread of one's choice (We went with brioche bread)

Directions

  1. Crack eggs and pour milk into deep baking pan
  2. Add sugar and cinammon to pan and then whisk everything together
  3. Dip a slice of bread into pan, making sure to do both sides of the slice
  4. Fry in a greased/nonstick frying pan until golden brown, making sure to flip occasionally
