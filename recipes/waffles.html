Waffles

Image Source

Description: Scrumptious waffles coated in your choice of syrup

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Cooking spray for the waffle iron

Directions

  1. Crack and whisk eggs
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients (except the cooking spray) into the eggs and continue whisking
  3. After enough whisking use batter in a waffle iron
  4. After waffles are cooked serve with syrup and choice of toppings
